Baiterek

Speaking about the sights of Kazakhstan, we cannot but tell you about bayterek - one of the main symbols of the country, representing its prosperity and well-being. by design architects, this landmark of Kazakhstan embodied in imagine the idea of ​​a three-level World Tree, symbolizing antagonism good and evil, and uniting the world of the dead, the world of the living and heaven. IN the lower level of the building is a cafe, art gallery and a large aquarium with fish. Monument middle level is a high tower with modern high-speed elevators. The upper level is designed in the form of a huge glass ball, from the inside of which a beautiful panoramic view opens

Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi

Other attractions include guides in Kazakhstan recommend visiting this most important shrine on the endless steppes South Kazakhstan. The mausoleum was built in honor of the famous poet Ahmed Yasawi in 1405 in the place where earlier located his modest grave. Over time this architectural ensemble combined a variety of premises - tomb, mosque, library, pantheon of Kazakh khans from 43 tombstones, a bathhouse and various ancillary facilities.

Baikonur Cosmodrome

For many decades, the Baikonur Cosmodrome has been remains the world's most important space launch site. aircraft. This is where the first flight took place. man into space, the launch of the first artificial satellite, famous manned spacecraft and orbital stations, as well as other spacecraft, to this day plowing the expanses solar system. There is no doubt what to visit in Kazakhstan, the Baikonur Cosmodrome is among the first places - this the trip will leave the most extraordinary memories.

Charyn Canyon

The nature of Kazakhstan is diverse, and this diversity complements picturesque 154-kilometer gorge - Charyn Canyon, stretching along the valley of the Charyn River. It is this river that several million years carved a gorge in the rock, creating a unique landscape in this area. canyon today has a relief that surprises with its diversity - bizarre shapes rocks, steep slopes, plumb lines, hills, arches and columns attention and resemble certain sculptures-statues.

Borovoe

Akmola region is distinguished by the extraordinary beauty of the landscape and comfortable climate. Therefore, this is where the resort is located. Borovoe, often called the “pearl of Kazakhstan”. It is a beautiful oasis with blue lakes in framed by high mountains and centuries-old pines. Resort sanatoriums offer a whole range of health services - mud therapy, balneological procedures, treatment in salt mines, radon therapy, etc.

The huge 150-meter "tent" of the shopping center can already be seen from afar, organically fitting into the architecture of the surrounding structures. Once inside, you can spend almost the whole day - so many interesting things are concentrated on the squares of Khan-Shatyr. This numerous shops, restaurants, Internet cafes, green greenhouse, a huge parking lot and even a large water park with three swimming pools and a real sandy “beach”.

National park Altyn-Emel

The largest protected area in Kazakhstan, national park Altyn-Emel is located in the desert and mountainous regions of the country. It is here that the famous Singing Dune is located, unusual the Katutau and Aktau mountains, as well as the well-known Saka burial mounds. This land attracts with amazing landscapes, purest mountain air and numerous monuments of antiquity. Another one attraction of the park - radon springs with warm healing waters.

Lake Balkhash

The largest lake in the Republic of Kazakhstan is Lake Balkhash, stretching for 600 km in length. Numerous beaches of the lake covered with sand and ideal for relaxing from May to September. home the unique feature of this lake is that its waters differ in composition: the water area of ​​the lake is divided into parts by a narrow strait, the western part of the lake is filled with fresh water, and the eastern - salty. Salt waters and mud of Balkhash have properties, beneficial to the human body.

Palace of Peace and Harmony

Returning to the question of what to see in Kazakhstan, once in its capital, we can recommend the Palace of Peace and Harmony - more one attraction of Kazakhstan from the category of unusual architectural structures. The building is made in a futuristic style in the form of a regular pyramid 62 meters high, the sides of which symbolize the openness of the Kazakh people to all nations and religions.
